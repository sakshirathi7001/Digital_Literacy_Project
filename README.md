# Digital_Literacy_Project
The repository contains my Digital Literacy project, where I created an infographic to explain concepts like digital literacy, safe internet practices, and maintaining a professional online presence. It also includes other tasks related to digital tools, communication, and cyber awareness.

##  Project Structure

* task-1-presentation → Infographic on digital literacy
* task-2-portfolio → Screenshots of my profiles
* task-3-platforms → Coding platform + Google form
* task-4-email-etiquette → Emails and checklist
* task-5-cybercrime → Case study and prevention tips
* report → Final project report

##  Task Overview

###  Task 1 – Infographic

I created a simple infographic using Canva. It includes topics like digital literacy, safe internet practices and email etiquette. I tried to keep it simple so anyone can understand easily.

---

###  Task 2 – Digital Portfolio

In this task, I created my accounts on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn and Kaggle. These platforms are useful for building professional profile and showing skills.

---

###  Task 3 – Platforms

I solved coding problems on HackerRank and also created a Google Form quiz on digital literacy. This helped me understand how online tools can be used for learning.

---

###  Task 4 – Email Etiquette

I wrote two professional emails and created a list of do’s and don’ts for social media. This task helped me understand how to communicate properly online.

---

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

I studied about cybercrime and created a case study with prevention tips. It helped me understand online risks and how to stay safe.

---

## What I Learned

Through this project, I learned about digital tools, online safety, communication and cyber awareness. It also helped me to improve my presentation and understanding skills.
